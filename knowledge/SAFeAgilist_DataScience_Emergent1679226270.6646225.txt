What about communication skills? How can I improve in that area?

Tim:
Communication skills are vital for a data scientist, as you'll need to be able to explain complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders. Some ways to improve your communication skills include practicing presenting your work to others, participating in public speaking events, and writing blog posts or research papers. Additionally, seeking feedback from others is crucial to improving your communication skills; consider finding a mentor or joining a peer review group.
