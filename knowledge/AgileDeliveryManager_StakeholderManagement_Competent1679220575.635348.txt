Tim: Stakeholder Management is a key competency for Agile Delivery Managers as it involves managing stakeholder expectations, identifying and engaging various stakeholders, and ensuring that their needs and requirements are met. The skills necessary for Stakeholder Management include active listening skills, conflict resolution skills, negotiation skills, and communication skills. 

In terms of attributes, being a good stakeholder manager requires having empathy, understanding, and respect towards each stakeholder group. In addition, you must be able to maintain a positive relationship with each stakeholder and be able to manage expectations and conflicts in a professional manner. 

As for practices, stakeholder mapping, stakeholder analysis, and stakeholder engagement planning are all important practices in the stakeholder management process.
