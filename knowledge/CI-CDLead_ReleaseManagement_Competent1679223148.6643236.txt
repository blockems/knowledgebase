The skills needed to be a competent Release Manager for CI/CD Lead include effective communication, leadership, and project management. You need to be able to manage timelines, collaborate with stakeholders, and understand technical details. Additionally, you should have experience in designing, implementing, and monitoring release policies, as well as testing and deploying released software. 

Some key attributes that are important for Release Management include attention to detail, strong analytical skills, and the ability to think strategically. In terms of practices, it is important to follow industry best practices and standards such as ITIL or DevOps, and gain a deep understanding of the different software development life cycles. You should have experience in automating releases and testing processes, and working with different tools such as Jenkins, Git, and Docker. 

To be a fully competent Release Manager, you should also have a solid understanding of cloud computing platforms and the security implications of CI/CD. It is important to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in the field, as well as attending conferences and participating in networking events.
