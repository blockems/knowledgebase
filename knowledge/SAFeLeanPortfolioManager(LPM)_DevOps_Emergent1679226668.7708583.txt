User: Can you start with the skills that are needed?

Tim: Sure! The skills that are essential for being at the Emergent level of DevOps competency for an LPM can be grouped into three categories: technical, business, and leadership skills. Technical skills include understanding infrastructure, coding, and automation. Business skills include a good knowledge of agile methodologies, business analysis, and product management. Leadership skills, on the other hand, involve the ability to motivate and influence others while making decisions that keep the business objectives in mind.
